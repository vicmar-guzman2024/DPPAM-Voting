Mission & Vision

Our Mandate

As the citizen’s arm of the Commission on Elections, PPCRV’s mandates include the following:
  • To continuously conduct voters' education nationwide, especially in far-flung areas not reached by Comelec;
  • To monitor the conduct of the elections (poll watching) and assist the voting populace in every polling precinct in the country; and
  • To conduct an unofficial parallel count of the electronic results. This is the centerfold of the PPCRV Command Center.

Mission

PCNC is committed to enabling NGOs to amplify their mission through a credible and accessible accreditation system.

Vision

A trusted and self-regulating community of NGOs, participating effectively in nation-building.
