Resoundingly, the most important characteristic is MAKA-DIYOS, being God-loving and God-Fearing. A Model Filipino should also be MATAPAT, MAGALANG, MASIPAG, MATULUNGIN, MAKABAYAN. PPCRV created 1 book for each of these characteristics, with MAKA-DIYOS prefacing each of the books. Quoting de Villa-Singson “…God cannot be contained in one book because He is the beginning and end of “all things visible and the evidence of things unseen” (Hebrews)…As such, a chapter on God and our faith prefaces each and every Tibok Pinoy book."

CBCP PRESIDENT H.E. ARCHBISHOP AMBO DAVID'S TALK AT THE PPCRV LAUNCH OF TIBOK PINOY on September 10 at the Dusit Thani Hotel. Archbishop David enjoined everyone present to participate in Voters Empowerment by promoting Tibok Pinoy's books. He said that PPCRV's TIBOK PINOY, a values development program of 6 books and 11 podcasts which focus on values to develop the Model Filipino is a testament to the 2nd Plenary Council of the Philippines which invited lay people and organizations to serve society as members of the Church.
